NO COST EMI

No Cost EMI on Flipkart is a payment option that allows customers to buy products and pay in monthly installments without any additional interest or extra charges. The total cost of the product is divided into equal EMIs, making it easier to afford high-value items like smartphones, appliances, and electronics. There’s no processing fee, and the amount you pay in EMIs equals the actual product price. It’s available on select products and can be availed using credit/debit cards or through partner financial services. No Cost EMI makes shopping more convenient and budget-friendly.
